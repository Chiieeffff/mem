GATEWAY CRYPTO — B2B SALES & PARTNERSHIP BRIEF
================================================


WHAT IT IS
----------
Gateway Crypto is a crypto payment processing platform for high-risk businesses.
It handles two use cases:

- C2B (Consumer-to-Business): Merchants collecting payments from end users and
  making payouts — the main use case
- B2B: Corporate on-ramp/off-ramp, manual large transactions, invoicing,
  exchange capabilities


KEY DIFFERENTIATORS (YOUR SALES ARGUMENTS)
-------------------------------------------

1. Network-agnostic USDT/USDC — the killer pitch
Most competitors charge for cross-chain swaps just like any exchange. Gateway
doesn't. No cross-chain swap fees, no manual operations, no risk of running out
of liquidity on a specific chain at 4am when your players can't withdraw.
If the prospect understands crypto at all, this lands hard.

2. Fully customizable role management
Merchants can mirror their existing security protocols — e.g., "any transaction
above $X requires owner approval." They don't have to adapt their compliance
rules to fit the crypto processor. It fits them.

3. Track record
- Never hacked
- 99.9% uptime
- Zero merchant churn in 18 months — no one has left for a competitor

4. Speed to launch
Up and running within 24 hours if the merchant is responsive.
KYB and integration can run in parallel.

5. Full client support
Dedicated account manager + 24/7 integration support throughout the entire
client lifecycle.

6. Simplified KYC for end users
First deposit requires simplified KYC, but after that users can transact up to
$10K without additional KYC. Good pitch for gaming/forex clients with high
player volumes.


TARGET CLIENTS
--------------

Direct merchants:
- iGaming / Sports Betting — high volume, take all comers, it's a numbers game
- Licensed Forex — lower volume risk, higher quality leads, requires some
  substance (not cover-up operations)
- High-risk service businesses — marketing agencies, lead gen, VPN-adjacent
  businesses that can't access traditional banking rails
- Adult — growing traction, not a core focus

Channel partners (B2B partnership angle):
- iGaming platforms that white-label or aggregate merchants under them.
  If Gateway integrates at the platform level, all sub-merchants get access
  automatically. This is the partnership play.


GEO & COMPLIANCE RULES
-----------------------
- No USA (no US-registered businesses, no merchants targeting US retail,
  no US transactions)
- No China
- No OFAC-sanctioned countries — edge cases handled practically, not rigidly
  (e.g., dual-passport owners, Curacao entities are fine)
- They do practical due diligence, not just paperwork. They can spot cover-up
  operations.
- They're fine with high-risk businesses presenting themselves as-is.
  A storefront in crypto actually raises red flags, not lowers them.

PRICING STRUCTURE (talking points, not a sheet)
------------------------------------------------
They don't distribute PDFs with fixed pricing — it circulates in the market.
Share verbally or in a private message. The framework:

- Deposit (no exchange): Base fee
- Deposit with auto-exchange (user pays in altcoin, merchant accumulates USDT):
  Slightly higher
- Manual internal exchange (merchant swaps BTC → USDT in dashboard):
  Separate fee
- Withdrawal: Free — only network fee charged; fees are collected on deposit side
- Withdrawal with exchange (user withdraws in different currency than deposited):
  Same as deposit rate
- C2B on-ramp cost: Passed to end user, net-zero for the merchant
- B2B on-ramp/off-ramp: Negotiated per client

Key framing: when a prospect says "I'm paying 0.4% for crypto processing" —
they're almost certainly quoting deposit-only. Push them to compare full-cycle
cost, same way you'd compare fiat processors.


PARTNERSHIP PROCESS
--------------------
1. You identify a merchant and drop: business type, needs, geos into a shared
   Telegram channel
2. Gateway pre-approves in chat (fast filter, avoids wasted time)
3. They create a group with your client and handle KYB + integration from there
4. You can stay in the chat or step out — your call

Revenue share options:
- Gateway sets up an account for you on their platform and settles your cut
  directly in crypto
- Monthly/bi-weekly reconciliation invoice paid in crypto
- Automated referral dashboard coming ~Q3 (real-time fee tracking)
